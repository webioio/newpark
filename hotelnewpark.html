A quiet address on the Boulevard

Hotel New Park sits on Srinagar's Boulevard road, on the edge of Dal Lake, in the heart of a city built around water. Eighty-eight rooms, a multi-cuisine restaurant, and a welcome that hasn't changed much since the hotel's earlier decades.

Eleven kilometres from Srinagar airport and walking distance to the lake, Lal Chowk, and the Mughal Gardens — with Gulmarg, Pahalgam and Sonmarg all within about two hours by road.
